How to Use:

  • Highlight Text: Highlight the text on any webpage, then click the Talk-To-Me extension. It will automatically start reading the highlighted text aloud.
  • Pause and Resume: Click on the text area in the popup to pause and resume the speech at any time.
  • Support the Developer: If you enjoy using Talk-To-Me, consider supporting the developer by buying them a cup of coffee. Your support helps maintain and improve the extension!
